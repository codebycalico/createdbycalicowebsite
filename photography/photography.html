about me

Creating art is a way to express the emotions and feelings I have that I don't have words for. I'm inspired by small moments, by sunlight leaking through leaves, by a warm breeze, by seeing strangers in love, by seeing someone feeling lonely, by shadows and darkness and by light, by the look in someone's eye when they feel like they're truly seen.
